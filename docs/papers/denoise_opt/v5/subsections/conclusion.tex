\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

DenoiseOpt casts wavetable wrap discontinuity as cycle-local seam repair under a prolonged residual $R$, with a hybrid GA--PPO(+PBT) outer loop over bake cells.
At the overnight freeze the champion reaches $R\approx 0.9909$ versus DualCosine $\approx 0.8166$.
On the frozen holdout a compact favorite reaches $R\approx 0.991$ versus DualCosine $R\approx 0.825$; across twenty multi-family draws, mean $\Delta R\approx{+}0.162$ (Wilcoxon $p\approx 8.9{\times}10^{-5}$).
Scope remains wavetable seam restoration.
