# ReelSynth

## Product name

ReelSynth

## Positioning

Open-source wavetable synthesizer for hands-on sound design — import, sculpt, and play Vital-class patches without leaving your studio.

ReelSynth is the **instrument**, not the agent. It is MIT-licensed DSP you can run standalone or as a CLAP/VST3/AU plugin. Reeldemo’s agent, text-to-wavetable, and compose integration are commercial and live separately.

## One-liner

**Sculpt wavetables. Route modulation. Play in your DAW.**

## Audience

- Producers who want manual WT control, not one-shot AI output
- Sound designers importing Vital, Serum, and WAV cycles
- Open-source audio developers extending the Rust engine

## Brand attributes

## Voice and tone

Short, direct, verb-forward: **sculpt**, **morph**, **route**, **play**, **export**.

Confident without hype. No “creativity unlocked” clichés. No mystical AI framing.
